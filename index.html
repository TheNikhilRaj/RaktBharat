Our Vision and Mission

VISION

To save people's blood without facing any issues and make every life precious and priceless.

MISSION

An organization for those who exploit those in need by selling blood bags at inflated prices. We ensure a transparent, direct, and free connection between donors and recipients.

The Challenges We Solve: Ending Exploitation and Delay

Broker Exploitation

Brokers are like parasites, exploiting desperate situations and turning a life-saving necessity into a business. Rakt Bharat provides a free alternative.

Precious Time Lost

Many patients lose their lives because they don't have the time to search for the right blood group or a suitable donor in an emergency.
